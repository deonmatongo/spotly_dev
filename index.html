One backend. Five connected surfaces.

      Included

      In this 8‑week build
      • All Phase 1–4 features across the five surfaces
      • One iOS and one Android app (single React Native codebase) for Customer & Driver
      • Web Business Dashboard and web Admin Dashboard
      • Payments: EcoCash, OneMoney, Visa/Mastercard, bank transfer
      • Push, SMS and email notifications
      • Testing/QA and debugging (Phases 5–6)

      Future roadmap

      Quoted separately as Phase 2
      • Concierge chat ("Plan my weekend") and AI recommendations
      • Group bookings, corporate accounts, membership subscriptions
      • Hotel bookings, travel experiences, car‑hire bookings
      • Private event bookings
      • Apple Pay / Google Pay — architecture ready, activation later

      Assumptions & notes

      How we keep it on time and on budget.

      What the client provides

      Branding assets, initial content (venues and events) and access to payment‑provider merchant accounts — EcoCash, OneMoney and a card acquirer.

      Third‑party costs

      Developer accounts, payment‑gateway fees, SMS/push credits, map and API usage and hosting are billed at cost or covered directly by the client.

      Timely feedback

      The timeline assumes prompt client feedback at each milestone. User Acceptance Testing in Week 7 needs client availability to sign off.

      Fixed scope

      Scope is fixed to this document. New features are handled with a simple change‑request and may affect the timeline.
